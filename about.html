Our Story

A theater-first team

TicketNest was built by a small team of moviegoers who were tired of clunky booking sites. We stripped out everything that got in the way — slow checkouts, confusing seat charts, accounts you didn't want to make — and kept only what actually helps you get to your seat faster.

Today we partner with theaters running everything from packed opening-night blockbusters to quiet Tuesday matinees, and we're just getting started.
