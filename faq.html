Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know before you book.

How do I book a ticket?

Choose a movie from the Now Showing list, pick a showtime, select your seats on the seat map, and confirm. You'll get a booking summary with your seat numbers and total.

Can I change my seats after booking?

Once a booking is confirmed, seats are locked in. If you need a change, contact the theater directly before showtime and they'll do their best to help.

Is there a booking fee?

Ticket prices shown at checkout are the full price — no hidden fees added at the last step.

What if a showtime sells out?

Seats marked "Taken" on the seat map are no longer available. Check the next showtime for the same movie, usually listed right alongside it.

Do I need to create an account?

No — you can browse and book without signing up. An account is only useful if you want to save your booking history.

Can I get a refund?

Refund policies vary by theater. Reach out through our Contact page with your booking details and we'll point you in the right direction.
